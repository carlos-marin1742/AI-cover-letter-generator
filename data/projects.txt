NeuroScan AI: A YOLO-based brain MRI classifier with a Node.js/Express 
backend and React frontend, deployed on Render. Classifies MRI scans 
using computer vision for medical imaging applications.

AI Cover Letter Generator: Flask app using LangChain and Google Gemini 
to generate tailored cover letters from resume PDFs and job descriptions.

AI Stock Advisor: Streamlit app featuring Bollinger Bands, RSI, and 
momentum tracking for stock analysis.

AI Image Generator: React frontend with Hugging Face Inference API, 
deployed as a static site on Render.
